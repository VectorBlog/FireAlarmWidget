North Hollywood High School Fire Alarms

Unfortunately, after over 4 years of alarm data collection from December 2016 to March 2021 and a total of 175 alarms, the alarm monitoring device no longer exists. The data here has been archived for informational purposes, but will no longer update unless the device is fixed at some point in the future. Thanks for joining us on our quest to quantify the issue of fire alarm interruptions at NHHS.
